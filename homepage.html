Care for Climate

An environmental charity working to a brighter future

We are a charity that works to help our planet and the people and animals who love on it before it is too late. Our goal is to stablise the increasing temperature of the planet by reducing our C02 levels to below the carbon neutral level.


As you can see from the graph there has been a sharp increase in temperature over the last 200 years this is due to many factors:
  • Increase in population
  • Industrial revolutions in many countries
  • Increased standard of living

'We are living on the planet like we have another one to go to'
Every year we are taking a step towards irreversible damage to our home planet. A common misconception is that we will always have time to change and that it is someone else's problem. This is wrong and irresponsible. The harsh reality is that every increase in CO2 causes a slight increase in the temperature causes some of the icebergs to melt. This is not just bad for the polar bears it is bad for you as well; icebergs contain methane which will further cause the increase in temperature.
